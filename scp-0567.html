Item #: SCP 0567

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures:

Due to the nature of Scp-0567, containment is currently considered impossible. No known physical
barriers, countermeasures, or memetic strategies have successfully prevented manifestation. Foundation
protocol dictates that all reported sightings are to be discredited publicly as hallucinations, stress-induced
delusions, or urban legends.

Victims identified as being targeted by SCP-0567 are to be placed under observation for research
purposes, though removal or protection has proven ineffective. Class-B amnestics are ineffective; instances
of psychological trauma remain.

Foundation webcrawlers are tasked with scrubbing social media and news reports for keywords relating to
“being watched,” “someone in the corner,” or similar phenomena for incident tracking.

Description:

SCP-0567 refers to a phenomenon in which humanoid entities, colloquially called “Watchers,” manifest in
the peripheral vision of individuals. These entities:

  • Appear as vague human-like silhouettes. Victims universally describe them as "ordinary people" yet
    with an unnerving, unnatural stillness.

  • Manifest in dimly lit locations: corners of streets, alleyways, abandoned hallways, or aisles in otherwise
    populated public areas.

  • Are never fully visible. When the victim attempts direct focus, the entity withdraws back into the corner
    and disappears instantly.

  • Appear to exhibit selective targeting. Victims are chosen seemingly at random, though once chosen,
    manifestations increase in frequency until the subject’s death.

Psychological Effects:


Prolonged exposure results in:
  • Paranoia (reports of “eyes always on me”).

  • Insomnia and anxiety disorders.

  • Progressive obsession with the entities — many victims attempt to photograph or confront them,
    always unsuccessfully.

  • speaking in unknown eldritch language

A subset of victims exhibit accelerated psychological deterioration, culminating in violent behavior, hallucinations, or self-harm.

SCP-0567 does not appear to directly kill targets. Instead, it is hypothesized that the entities “wait” until the
subject’s death by natural or indirect causes (suicide, accidents, stress-induced illness). After death,
manifestations cease entirely for the victim and redirect to a new subject.
