Location: Site-77, Containment Interview Room 3

SCP-3421-1- ██ was the only known instance to retain full speech, memory, and cognitive function following transformation. Initially believed to be recoverable, the subject's growing influence over nearby personnel and containment staff led to reassessment. Interview was permitted under controlled conditions with failsafes in place. Subject was terminated less than 24 hours later.

[BEGIN LOG]

Dr. Rylin: Good afternoon. Please state your name for the record.

SCP-3421-1-██: I had a name once. You already know it. But names are soft. Flesh is soft. It rots. I am not soft anymore.
